Pharisee academy International Academy was founded in BC117, on the First day of the month of Adar,in the towm of Ramathaim, of the state of Ephraim.
His excellency ,King Herod I ,graced the inauguration ceremony of Pharisee Academy International Network.

Professor Nicodemus Jonah,the great granfather of Josephus, the famous historian spoke at the inauguration, exhorting the students to live up to the prestigous name of PAIN.
